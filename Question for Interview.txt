Interview Question For DBA

1 What types of databases do you work with?

2 Do you have experience with on-premises databases, cloud databases or both?

3 Can you explain what ODBC is?

4 What is the highest number of database servers you have worked with?

5 How would you handle data loss during a database migration?

6 Give an example of a goal you reached and tell me how you achieved it.

7 Give an example of a goal you didn't meet and how you handled it. 

8 Describe a stressful situation at work and how you handled it.

9 Tell me about how you worked effectively under pressure.
          How do you handle a challenge?
